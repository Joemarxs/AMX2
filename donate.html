Why Donate?

At AMX we are committed to transforming the lives of street children. Every day, countless children face the harsh realities of homelessness, poverty, and abandonment. Your donation can make a significant difference in providing them with essential resources, education, and a chance for a brighter future.

How Your Donation Helps.

Your generous contribution enables us to: Provide Shelter and Safety: Ensure that street children have a safe place to stay, away from dangers of the streets. Offer Nutritious Meals: Provide regular meals to ensure their health and well-being. Access to Education: Support their education through tuition fees, school supplies, and educational programs. Medical Care: Ensure access to necessary medical treatment and vaccinations. Counseling and Support: Offer emotional support and counseling to help them overcome trauma and build resilience.
